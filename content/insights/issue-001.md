## Who EterSolis Is

EterSolis is an advanced resource recovery and circular economy organization focused on responsible resource stewardship, applied science and sustainable industrial solutions.

### Resource-First Thinking

Waste, water, materials and by-products can contain environmental, economic and operational value.

### Applied Science

Evidence, characterization and disciplined documentation matter more than slogans.

### Practical Stewardship

Solutions must respect safety, law, community benefit and commercial feasibility.

### Long-Term Value

EterSolis is built to help retain value, strengthen systems and support resilience over time.

> Founding position: Value should never be wasted.

## Why EterSolis Exists

A linear economy cannot sustain a circular planet. Modern society depends on water, materials, energy, infrastructure and resilient communities. Yet enormous quantities of value are still lost through disposal, contamination and underuse. EterSolis exists to help close that gap.

### Public Context

- 2.1B people lacked safely managed drinking water in 2024.
- 3.4B people lacked safely managed sanitation in 2024.
- 2.1B tonnes of municipal solid waste were generated in 2023.
- 3.8B tonnes of municipal solid waste generation are projected by 2050.

Key implication: global resource pressure creates a practical need for recovery, reuse and stronger systems thinking.

## What EterSolis Does

EterSolis is structured to connect science, stewardship and practical implementation across five public-facing domains.

### Industrial Systems

Improving process thinking, resource efficiency and sustainability in real operations.

### Water and Wastewater Systems

Supporting water stewardship, sanitation resilience and public-health-aware infrastructure conversations.

### Recyclables and Circular Materials

Helping materials stay in productive use through quality-aware circularity and responsible routing.

### Biological Systems

Exploring safe, evidence-based value pathways for organic streams and biological recovery.

### Business Development and Commercialisation

Connecting technical capability, partnerships and disciplined implementation.

EterSolis is designed as a problem-solving operating platform: commercially practical, technically cautious and focused on recoverable value.

## The EterSolis Resource Hierarchy

Value retention before residual treatment. EterSolis uses a high-level prioritization framework that screens higher-value pathways before lower-value residual routes, unless safety, environmental, technical, commercial or regulatory reasons justify exclusion.

1. Reuse — use materials again without reprocessing.
2. Recycling — reprocess materials into new products.
3. Resource recovery — recover valuable materials and nutrients.
4. Organics recovery — recover organics for beneficial applications.
5. Hydrocarbon recovery — recover hydrocarbons for productive use.
6. Energy recovery — recover energy through controlled conversion.
7. Final residue treatment — treat residuals safely and in compliance.
8. Biochar enhancements — enhance stability and sequestration potential.

Decision logic: reuse and material value first; lower-value treatment only when higher pathways are screened out or unsuitable.

## The EterSolis Approach

Five operating principles shape the public and internal discipline of the organization.

- Science-based: decisions are grounded in credible data, evidence and peer-reviewed knowledge.
- Systems thinking: interconnected trade-offs across people, ecosystems and infrastructure are considered.
- Circularity: solutions are designed to recover resources, minimize waste and keep materials in use longer.
- Practicality: EterSolis delivers reliable, operational solutions that work in the real world and create value.
- Long-term stewardship: EterSolis acts today for lasting impact, protecting resources for future generations.

### Our Process

1. Identify needs, opportunities and context.
2. Characterize data and assess the problem.
3. Prioritize impacts, risks and feasibility.
4. Design solutions that are effective, efficient and safe.
5. Implement with rigor, in partnership and at scale.
6. Measure performance and validate outcomes.
7. Improve continuously through learning and adaptation.

> Recover value responsibly, explain clearly and execute with discipline.

# EterSolis Executive Review Instructions

This document defines how executive reviewers should review the EterSolis website before merge, deployment or public release.

## Review Objective

Confirm that the website is professional, commercially useful, visually polished, technically credible and aligned with EterSolis controlled public communication standards.

## What To Review

Review these areas on desktop and mobile:

- Homepage
- Sell Waste
- Solutions
- Industries
- About
- Contact
- Insights
- Helios
- Media Credits
- Privacy
- Terms
- Header, footer and navigation
- Light and dark mode
- Public forms and notices
- Executive previews and generated previews

## Brand Review

Confirm:

- EterSolis is spelled as one word.
- Official transparent logo assets are used.
- Logos do not have background boxes.
- Dark logo is used on light backgrounds.
- Light logo is used on dark backgrounds.
- Brand colors remain Coal Grey, Sunshine Yellow, Aero White, Cool Gray and Carbon Black.
- Visual design is modern and polished, not plain text on whitespace.

## Messaging Review

Confirm:

- EterSolis is described as a privately owned waste and carbon management company.
- Public language emphasizes practical problem solving, service reliability, responsible resource recovery and environmental protection.
- Copy avoids unsupported impact claims, greenwashing and public-authority positioning.
- Copy does not disclose protected systems, formulas, pricing logic, confidential partners or internal process details.

## Waste Submission Review

Confirm:

- Sell Waste remains highly visible.
- The waste submission notice is clearly non-binding.
- The site does not imply acceptance, purchase, transport approval, disposal instruction, quote or contract.
- The site instructs users not to send physical samples unless EterSolis gives written intake instructions.
- Hazardous, regulated, biological, chemical, medical, radioactive or unknown material language is cautious and routes to review.

## Visual Quality Review

Confirm every page has:

- strong hero section;
- clear CTA path;
- professional cards or visual sections;
- consistent spacing and hierarchy;
- mobile-friendly composition;
- readable contrast in light and dark mode;
- no generic startup filler visuals;
- no landfill shock imagery;
- no empty pages with only text lists.

## Functional Review

Confirm:

- navigation links resolve;
- anchors work;
- mobile menu opens and closes;
- light/dark toggle works;
- forms are labeled and structured;
- footer links resolve;
- Media Credits page lists source records;
- Helios is guided routing only, not unrestricted AI advice.

## Approval Decision

Record one decision in the pull request:

- Approved for production release
- Approved subject to copy changes
- Approved subject to visual changes
- Hold: legal/privacy review required
- Hold: technical QA required

Include specific page names and requested changes when approval is conditional.

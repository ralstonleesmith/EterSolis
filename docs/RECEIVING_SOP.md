# Receiving SOP

## Purpose

Define inbound material receiving after pickup or delivery.

## Rules

- Receiving captures arrival, condition, quantity, evidence, exceptions and operator notes.
- Public views must not reveal sensitive site logistics or operator notes.
- Received material must be assigned to a stockpile lot or controlled disposition path.

## Records

- `receiving_records`
- `stockpile_lots`
- `case_files`
- `case_events`

# Data Retention SOP

## Purpose

Define retention expectations for operational, commercial, payment and certificate records.

## Rules

- Case, quotation, invoice, payment reconciliation, certificate and audit records are permanent unless a lawful deletion workflow applies.
- Uploaded files follow configured storage retention and privacy rules.
- Deletion requests must preserve required legal, tax, audit and certificate evidence where retention is mandatory.

## Records

- `data_retention_jobs`
- `data_deletion_requests`
- `consent_records`
- `access_logs`

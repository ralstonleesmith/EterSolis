# Refund SOP

## Purpose

Control refunds for overpayment, cancelled scope or approved commercial adjustment.

## Rules

- Refunds must reference the case and, where possible, the invoice.
- Refunds require a reason, approval status and resolution date.
- Refund action must not delete payment, receipt or reconciliation history.

## Records

- `refunds`
- `case_events`
- `invoice_events`

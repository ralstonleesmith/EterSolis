# Quotation SOP

## Purpose

Ensure EterSolis documents commercial scope before invoice or payment.

## Rules

- Quotations must include scope, assumptions, exclusions, validity, currency, tax and approval status.
- Invoices are blocked until quote acceptance unless a documented fixed-fee exception applies.
- Revisions create new quotation events instead of mutating the commercial history silently.

## Records

- `quotations`
- `quotation_lines`
- `quotation_events`
- `quotation_acceptances`

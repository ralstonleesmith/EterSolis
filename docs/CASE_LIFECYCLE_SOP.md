# Case Lifecycle SOP

## Stages

1. Case submitted
2. Technical and commercial review
3. Quotation issued
4. Quotation accepted
5. Invoice issued
6. Payment proof uploaded
7. Payment reconciled
8. Pickup or delivery scheduled
9. Material received
10. Processing or disposition
11. Certificate decision
12. Case closed

## Rules

- A case is the operational spine for customer history, commercial records, files, scheduling, receiving, stockpile, processing and certificates.
- Each transition must write a `case_events` record.
- Closure requires commercial completion, operational disposition and certificate decision where applicable.

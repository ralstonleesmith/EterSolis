# Invoice SOP

## Purpose

Define invoice creation after quotation acceptance and before payment collection.

## Rules

- Invoices inherit accepted quotation scope.
- Payment references must be unique and visible to the customer.
- Receipts are issued only after reconciliation, not proof upload alone.
- Market-specific currency, timezone and payment method are stored with the invoice.

## Records

- `invoices`
- `invoice_lines`
- `invoice_events`
- `receipts`
